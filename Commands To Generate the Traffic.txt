Commands To Generate the Traffic:


Low Traffic:
ping google.com -n 20
nslookup google.com
curl https://example.com

Medium Traffic:
ping -4 google.com -n 200 -l 1000
nslookup youtube.com
curl https://google.com


High Traffic:
ping -4 8.8.8.8 -n 500 -l 2000
for /l %i in (1,1,50) do nslookup google.com
curl https://youtube.com
